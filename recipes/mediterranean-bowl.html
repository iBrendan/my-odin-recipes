Mediterranean Buddha Bowl

Description

This Culinary Hill inspired nourishing Mediterranean Buddha Bowl is full of colorful veggies, nutritious quinoa, and roasted chickpeas. Topped with hummus for an epic power lunch, could this be a taste of humanity's peak?

Ingredients

  • Roasted Chickpeas

    • 1 can chickpeas (14 oz)
    • 1 tbsp olive oil
    • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
    • 1/4 tsp dried basil
    • 1/4 tsp garlic powder

  • Quinoa

    • 1/2 cup uncooked quinoa, rinsed
    • 1 cup water

  • Salad

    • 2 cups mixed field greens or lettuce
    • 1 cup grape tomatoes halved
    • 2 cucumbers peeled, halved lengthwise and chopped
    • 1 yellow bell pepper stemmed, seeded, and chopped
    • 1/2 cup pitted Kalamata olives
    • 1/2 cup Hummus
