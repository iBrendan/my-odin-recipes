Chili Mango Zesty Buddha Bowl

Description

This Jar of Lemons inspired nourishing Buddha bowl with maple roasted chickpeas makes the perfect wholesome plant-based meal. Loaded with chickpeas, cherry tomatoes, and avocados, could anything be better?

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup quinoa, dry
  • 2 cups spinash, chopped
  • 1/2 cup black beans, canned
  • 1/4 cup corn, canned
  • 10 grape tomatoes
  • 1 mango
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp cayenne

Steps

  1. Cook the quinoa according to directions.
  2. While the quinoa is cooking, chop the spinach, slice the tomatoes, cut the mango and avocado.
  3. Once the quinoa has finished cooking, mix all of the ingredients together in a bowl except for the mango, chili powder, cayenne, and avocado.
  4. Top each salad with slices of avocado and mango, sprinkled with chili powder and cayenne.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
